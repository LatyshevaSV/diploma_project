Music Is Not My Job — It's My Language

IvyEvette is a DJ and music producer rooted in electronic and underground club culture. My sets move fluidly between deep, atmospheric textures and peak-time floor energy - never predictable, always intentional.

I has performed at underground venues, festivals, and private events across the region, and my original productions blend field recordings, synthesizers, and carefully layered percussion into something entirely me own.
